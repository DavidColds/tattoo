Aftercare

Wash you tattoo morning and evening, or/and when needed. Lubricate careful with a thin layer of aftercare cream/gel 3 times/day, or when it feels dry. Don't take too much, generally it's better to lubricate a little bit less than more. After some days there will form a thin crust that will crack and flake. It will sometimes look weird but itʼs fully normal. Continue to lubricate until all crust has came off. It usually takes 7-14 days. Make sure NOT to scratch or peel on the crust! It can result in infections, color loss and/or ugly scars that could be very hard to correct. Avoid solariums, pools, saunas and close contact exercises. Avoid movements that would make you feel ”tension” in the tattoo. Be careful with clothes and jewelry that could chafe. You will have thin skin bove your tattoo when the crust has came off. It will be replaced with normal skin durin 3 to 4 weeks. During this time you need to be careful to expose it to the sun. Now you can start lubricate with normal lubricate if you want.
